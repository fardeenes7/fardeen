Dokan was rebranded to 'Mahajon' recently.

## Description

A platform where micro-merchants can manage their business, inventory, Facebook Page and many more. 

## Features

### Current
- **Inventory Management**: Add, Edit, Delete products with ease.
- **Order Management**: View, Update, Delete orders.

### Upcoming
- **Facebook Page Integration**: Connect your Facebook page and manage your business from one place.
- **Invoice and Payment Management**: Generate invoices and manage payments.
- **E-commerce Website Builder**: One-click e-commerce website builder powered by Next.JS and Vercel Domains API that offers customization.
